USER GUIDE

THE GUIDE
NO ONE READS

But you're here. Smart. Let's get you lifting instead of clicking through some overpriced fitness app.

1
✏️

Paste your plan. That's it.

Hit the ✏️ pencil button at the top. A text box appears. Paste your workout in plain text — any format, any language. The app figures it out. Like a personal trainer, but without the small talk.

💡 Double blank line = new tab. Each block becomes a separate workout day.
2
🗂️

Tabs are magic.

Each workout block becomes a tab at the top. Day names like יום שני or Monday show as short names. Sequential workouts like Workout A show as a short label. No plan fits on a single day anyway — unless you're a masochist.

💡 A tiny 2/8 counter appears on each tab. Watch it fill up. It's the best part.
3
✅

Done a set? Hit "Done".

Each exercise card has a סיימתי button. Tap it when you're done. A 90-second rest timer starts automatically — with a ring, a countdown, and a beep at the end. You can add +30s or skip it entirely if you're either a hero or in a hurry.

⏱️ The beep is real. Don't be startled in a quiet gym.
4
⚖️

Track your weight.

Each card has a משקל ▼ dropdown. Pick your weight in kg. It's saved automatically. Hit 📈 to see your weight history — a bar chart with your last 10 sessions. Watch those bars grow. That's the whole point.

5
🔗

Supersets — for the overachievers.

Add a line that says סופר-סט or superset before a group of exercises. They'll get a gold border and a badge. The app respects your ambition. Your muscles won't.

6
🔥

Power week — when you're feeling heroic.

Use שבועות 1-3 and שבוע 4 formats in your plan. A toggle appears — switch between weeks 1–3 and Power Week. More sets, heavier reps, questionable life decisions. Your call.

7
▶️

YouTube, one tap away.

Every exercise card has a ▶ button. Tap it and YouTube opens with a search for that exercise. No excuses for bad form. Well — fewer excuses.

8
📲

Install it. It's free. Obviously.

Open the app in Chrome on Android. A banner appears at the bottom: "Add to Home Screen". Tap Install. Done — it lives on your phone like a real app. No App Store. No $0.99. No subscriptions. Just a file that works.

🍎 On iPhone: Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen.
